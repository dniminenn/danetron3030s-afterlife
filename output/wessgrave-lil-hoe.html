Lil Hunters

Airdropped to Hunters of Evil holders who burned both male and female parents. A generative Collection of 115 Lil Hunters, still learning and searching vengeance for their lost parents. Located on the FTM blockchain.

Benefits / Utility

There are 4 different levels that can be achieved, depending on how many Lil Hunters you adopted.

1. Lil Hunter 1 → hold 1 Lil Hunter
- Gain access to lil-hunters
- opens the door to the bloody wessgrave wars
2. Lil Hunter 2 → hold 2 Lil Hunters
3. Lil Hunter 3 → hold 3 Lil Hunters
4. Lil Hunter 4 → hold 4 Lil Hunters
