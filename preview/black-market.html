Reaching far inside the gloom of death, down beneath the scarred lands where millions of the dead roam, lies the strangest of places. Under the city of Myr lies a dark trader's paradise, a bazaar of the haunted, and the shop of the damned. This is the Afterlife Black Market. Here, anyone can purchase or trade anything. Be it small complex, eerie jewelry, creepy tinctures, peculiar minerals, bizarre creations and body parts, or even obscure and rare artifacts of the undeath. This shop of the deranged has it all. Bargains, deals, and souls, the Afterlife Black Market has just what you're looking for and it's open for eternity.
